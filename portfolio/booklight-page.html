Corner Booklight

A stylish triangular booklight that fits the corner of any book.

Bookmark Booklight

This booklight is shaped like a bookmark with a long stem. It can easily be inserted between the pages of a book.

Rectangle Booklight

This rectangular booklight can be placed along the edges of a booklight and can be slid up and down along the edge.

Problem

The booklights currently in the market are solid objects with a long slender stem that sticks out of the book. Most of these lights are point sources with uneven light distribution. These booklights are not plush with the plane of the book and are not pleasing to the eye.
