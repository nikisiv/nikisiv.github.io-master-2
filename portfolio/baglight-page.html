Bag light

Ever felt annoyed because you couldn't find that tiny coin from the dark depths of your bag? Baglight to the rescue: A teabag inspired light that can be 'dipped' like a teabag in handbags and backpacks to help fish out things better.

Problem

Often, we encounter situations when it gets difficult to pull out the right thing from our bag. In such situations, a bag light might come in handy - providing some visibility to the user and helping to find the right object in the bag.

The Product

The baglight in use

The baglight is designed to fulfil the following criteria: portability, simplicity, easy fabrication.
