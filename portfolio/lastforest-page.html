Redesigning reports and posters for Last Forest

Last Forest Enterprises promotes agro-ecological products and indigenous crafts. It primarily aims to bring value added produce from small farmers and harvesters into the market. It is based in the Nilgiris, India, and also provides a platform for many groups from around the country to be able to access the market.
